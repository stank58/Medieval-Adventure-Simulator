How To Play

Medieval Adventure Simulator is a text based game that allows you to progress from Peasant to Emperor by gaining fame and fortune.

To progress in the medieval heirarchy you will need to gain fame by completing quests. You do this by pressing the "Complete Quest" button.

If you suceed you will get gold and fame. If you fail, you will get a small amount of fame but no gold. Once you reach a certain level of fame your will gain a new rank automatically.

Both success and failure will result in a loss of health. You can regain health by drinking a potion from the shop or purchasing any type of armour.

You can also go on special quests by purchasing either the sword or the shield from the shop. These cost a lot of gold but can result in much higher rewards.

The end goal is to reach 2000 fame and become the Emperor which is the final rank... or is it?
